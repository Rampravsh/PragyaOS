## 1. Composition Flow ([Features/index.tsx](file:///g:/PragyaOS/apps/web/src/pages/Features/index.tsx))

The Features walkthrough details coordinates paths through:
- **Hero**: Layout explaining the active workspace, with ctas buttons and study rooms illustration logs.
- **Experience Path**: Timeline detailing passive consumption vs active practice methods.
- **Core Features**: 8-card capability display highlighting tutoring, annotations, and controls.
- **AI Companion Showcase**: Split-screen showcasing real-time companion summaries and question answering sidebars.
- **Student Timeline**: Stepper highlighting the 6 milestones from discovery to sharing.
- **Instructor Curriculum Showcase**: Course builder mock layout demonstrating drag-and-drop units draft logs.
- **Organization Audit Panel**: Organization workspace showing compliance, training rosters, and audit reports.
- **Platform Design benefits**: Summary cards representing high-speed load times, access settings, and collaboration circles.
- **Statistics Counter**: Counter metric dashboard showing completion rates.
- **FAQ Accordion**: FAQ accordion drawer logs.
- **CTA Banner**: CTA checklist panel coordinates.

## 2. Aesthetics & Scrolling Overlaps

- **Paper Margins Overlap**: Subsequent page elements overlap preceding containers (`-mt-12 md:-mt-20`) to coordinate journal sheets scrolling.
- **Visual Foundation**: Inherits Cormorant Garamond headings, Manrope body text, cream background, and gold accents.
- **Mockup Diagrams**: Tilted white cards and custom sidebars (representing rosters, planning units, and chat sidebars) designed with vector graphics in the illustration slots.
- **Keyboard accessibility**: Supports keyboard navigation focus lines, clean headings hierarchy, and screen-readers attributes.
